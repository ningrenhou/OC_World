Introduction

Welcome to OC's World! Our website is a platform dedicated to original character creators, and is dedicated to helping people design an OC of their own, and be able to use their design in games, anime, or wherever they want in the future. Here you can learn the ways and means of creating OC's, and software and resources will be provided within the site, if needed, you can check out the contact information at the bottom, there is an account on the site and a place to communicate, welcome to come and play together!

What is OC

The full name of OC is “Original Character”, which refers to a virtual character designed and characterized by the creator himself, and is born and produced from the creator's imagination and creativity, who can decide on the OC's image, personality, origin, backstory, relationships, and worldview at his own discretion. The fun of creating OCs is that you can let your imagination run wild and create a world under your own pen, and in that world, the characters you create can live their lives as they please.
